The Lotus: A Symbol of Purity and Resilience

By Durgesh Ashok Bagul

The lotus flower, revered for its elegance and symbolism, holds a prominent place in various cultures and traditions. This aquatic plant is much more than just a beautiful bloom; it represents purity, enlightenment, and resilience across civilizations.

A Natural Marvel

he lotus grows in muddy waters, yet it emerges pristine and untainted. This remarkable characteristic has inspired countless metaphors about rising above adversities. Found predominantly in Asia, the lotus thrives in warm climates and is a staple in wetlands and shallow ponds.
